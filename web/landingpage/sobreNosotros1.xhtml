Información de nosotros

Mars For Everyone is a business idea that arises from seeing the market's need to wear a style of clothing for different seasons that are used in cities or trips, focusing mainly on the combination of current and traditional fashion trends in Colombia.

This idea is developed mainly because most clothing stores in the city are limited to offering a local and common fashion, so they do not give the market an innovative and striking style such as Colombian trends between women and men of any age.

Why choose us?

Products to offer

Mars For Everyone offers various garments of origin and unique style, that depends on your taste, addressing looks of different trends in Colombia, so you don't stay with the taste of just one.

Fashion market

The main buyers, according to the studies, are women and men ranging from the age of 15 to 45, showing a special interest in clothing of innovative origin.
